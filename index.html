About Me

Hello! My name is Miguel Garcia. A Data Scientist based in San Diego, California.

I enjoy using data driven approaches to explain the nature of things. I find it important to deliver valuable insights that are backed up by statistics and data. It is my passion to uncover the answer to major questions using data science in order to help with objective decision making.

Currently pursuing a Master of Science in Data Science at Johns Hopkins University. I graduated from the University of California at San Diego in March 2020 with a major in Cognitive Science specializing in Machine Learning and Neural Computation. Shortly after graduting from UCSD, I started working as a Data Scientist Consultant at NGoggle Diagnostics where I work with professors, researchers, and lab directors on important research projects that aim to improve sleep quality.
